SWB

SWB is an inside joke between 2 friends who love perfumes, movies, music, and making fun of "creatives".

What started off as an actual creative endeavor in 2022, but

never made it past a few posts to hype it up has been reimagined

as a platform for reviewing things we find interesting and want to share.

SWB is commited to not providing completely honest insights, making fun of

the things you love and increasing shareholder revenue.

SWB is wholly owned and controlled by its founders, who are over $600,000.00 in debt to BlackRock.
